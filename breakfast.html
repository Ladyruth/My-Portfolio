Food Truck Mobile App

Breakfast & Spice

A mobile application design created for a bustling food truck business, completely streamlining the ordering process and eliminating long physical queues.

Role

UI/UX Designer

Tools

Figma, FigJam

Duration

4 Weeks

Deliverables

Mobile App Prototype, User Flows

[Hero Mobile App Mockup: Breakfast & Spice screens floating on dark background]

The Problem

Food trucks naturally struggle with highly condensed rush hours. Customers often find themselves spending half of their short lunch breaks standing in line just to place an order, resulting in deep frustration and sometimes leading potential customers to walk away.

User Insights

Conversations with regulars revealed they value speed and absolute transparency on pickup times above all else. They want to comfortably browse the menu from their office, securely place the order, and only walk down to the truck right exactly when their food is hot and ready.

Project Goals

  • Create a fast, image-first menu browsing experience.
  • Implement a very clear and accurate 'Time to Pickup' tracking feature.
  • Ensure the checkout process can be completed in under 4 taps.

The Solution

A vibrant, appetite-inducing mobile app that puts the focus purely on food imagery and speed. Utilizing an ultra-fast one-click checkout flow and real-time push notifications for order status updates, ensuring the customer literally never waits in the cold.

Key Design Decisions

🍔 Visual-First Menu

When deciding on food quickly, people eat with their eyes. Extra screen real estate was given to high-quality photography instead of dense text descriptions.

⏱️ Live Order Tracking

The core value proposition of the app. A massive, clear, constantly updating status screen tells the user the exact minute they should start walking to the truck.

💳 Rapid Checkout

By heavily utilizing saved payment methods and Apple/Google Pay integrations, the friction points of manual entry were entirely removed.
