AI Language App

Converseo

Converseo is an AI-powered and community centered language learning app focused on solving the hardest problem most language learners face: actually speaking the language.

Role: UI/UX Designer

Tools: Figma, FigJam

Duration: 4 Weeks

Deliverables: App Prototype, User Flows, Design System

The Problem

Learning a new language is a journey that many embark on, but few complete. This is partly because a major part of learning a new language is actually practicing it, and most learners live in an environment where very few people speak the language they want to learn. The journey can also be isolating and lonely.
Current apps focus too heavily on vocabulary memorization rather than conversational application.

User Insights

I conducted both user interviews and surveys with some language learners and found some recurring themes:

  • Forgetting what was learnt because of lack of practice
  • Not having anyone to speak the language with
  • Feel entirely disconnected from real-world conversation

User Persona

Understanding the anxieties of an ambitious language learner to drive an empathetic design methodology.

Project Goals

  • Make personal learning easier using AI
  • Making the language learning process a little less lonely by leveraging the power of communities
  • Help people find language learning partners

The Solution

A mobile app that helps people learn faster with 3 major features: Ai chatbot with guided prompts, Stories to test understanding and comprehension, and communities to meet fellow learners and practice in reall life scenarios.

Final Designs

Take a look at the polished interface designed to eliminate the anxiety of learning a new language.

Key Design Decisions

Familiar Chat UI

A low-pressure chat interface that mimics popular messaging apps, bringing instant familiarity to the user

Guided Prompts

Guided prompts to help beginners who might not know how to start a conversation

Gentle Color Palette

Bright neon language apps often increase heart rate and urgency. I settled on muted, grounding colors to reinforce an environment of patience and calm.

Notty Terminal

Product design for a crypto launchpad project, to make complex token sales and decentralized finance accessible.

Role: UI/UX Designer

Tools: Figma

Duration: 3 Weeks

The Problem

Decentralized finance (DeFi) platforms and crypto launchpads often suffer from cluttered, highly technical interfaces that alienate new users and increase the risk of costly errors during high-stakes token sales. Information density is extremely high, and bad UI makes it unreadable.

User Insights

Users need extreme clarity on token metrics, vesting schedules, and contribution limits. The primary insight gathered was that trust in Web3 is built directly through transparency and a clean, bug-free visual layout that doesn't overwhelm the user with unnecessary charts.

User Persona

Identifying the key frustrations of a retail crypto investor who struggles with densely packed interfaces.

The Solution

A sleek, dark-mode focused terminal interface that categorizes complex blockchain data into bite-sized, readable widgets. The interface uses minimal but highly intentional color accents to safely guide the user's attention through transactions and portfolio management without visual exhaustion.

Crowdfunding Platform

Support Hive

A trust-driven crowdfunding platform built to help individuals and communities easily raise funds for causes that matter.

Role: UI/UX Designer

Context: Hacktoberfest (She Codes Africa)

Team: Cross-functional Hackathon Team

Deliverables: UI/UX Design, Prototyping

This case study is coming soon. Click the button below to view the live product

View Live Site

The Problem

When people face emergencies or need to mobilize resources for community projects, navigating complex fundraising platforms can be overwhelming. Existing tools are often cluttered, charge high fees, or lack intuitive sharing features. Furthermore, donors hesitate to contribute if a platform's design fails to convey security and transparency.

User Insights

Trust is the highest currency in crowdfunding. Donors need to see clear visual indicators of credibility, such as transparent progress bars and verified campaign details. On the flip side, campaign creators require a frictionless setup process so they can focus entirely on telling their story and sharing it with their network.

User Personas

Balancing the emotional needs of a campaign creator in distress with a donor's need for transparency and ease.

[Image Placeholder: Dual Personas - The Campaign Organizer & The Donor]

User Flow

Mapping the most frictionless journey from discovering a campaign to securely completing a donation in under a minute.

[Image Placeholder: User Flow Diagram for Donation Checkout]

Project Goals

  • Design a highly trustworthy, credible interface that encourages donations.
  • Simplify the campaign creation wizard so anyone can launch a fundraiser in minutes.
  • Implement clear visual cues for campaign progress, social proof, and emotional storytelling.

The Solution

Support Hive bridges the gap between those in need and those willing to help through a clean, reassuring UI. By prioritizing large, impactful imagery for storytelling and stripping down the payment process to its absolute essentials, the platform empowers users to raise funds effectively and transparently.

Final Designs

Key Design Decisions

🤝 Trust-Building UI

To reassure donors, I incorporated clean layouts, verified user badges, and prominent progress bars to build instant credibility and transparency around where funds are going.

👀 Frictionless Checkout

Every additional step in a payment flow increases drop-off rates. I streamlined the donation process, allowing supporters to contribute securely with minimal clicks and cognitive load.

🎯 Empowering Storytelling

People donate to causes they connect with emotionally. The campaign pages prioritize large cover visuals and readable, well-spaced typography to ensure the creator's story takes center stage.

Food Truck Mobile App

Breakfast & Spice

A mobile application design created for a food truck business, to streamline the ordering process and eliminating long physical queues.

Role: UI/UX Designer

Tools: Figma, FigJam

Duration: 4 Weeks

Deliverables: Mobile App Prototype, User Flows

This case study is coming soon. Click the button below to view the prototype

View prototype

The Problem

Food trucks naturally struggle with highly condensed rush hours. Customers often find themselves spending half of their short lunch breaks standing in line just to place an order, resulting in deep frustration and sometimes leading potential customers to walk away.

User Insights

Conversations with regulars revealed they value speed and absolute transparency on pickup times above all else. They want to comfortably browse the menu from their office, securely place the order, and only walk down to the truck right exactly when their food is hot and ready.

[Image Placeholder: User Persona or Journey Map]
[Image Placeholder: Wireframes or Sketches]

Project Goals

  • Create a fast, image-first menu browsing experience.
  • Implement a very clear and accurate 'Time to Pickup' tracking feature.
  • Ensure the checkout process can be completed in under 4 taps.

The Solution

A vibrant, appetite-inducing mobile app that puts the focus purely on food imagery and speed. Utilizing an ultra-fast one-click checkout flow and real-time push notifications for order status updates, ensuring the customer literally never waits in the cold.
