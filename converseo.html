AI Language App

Converseo

Converseo is an AI-powered language learning app focused on solving the hardest problem most language learners face: actually speaking the language.

Tools

Duration

4 Weeks

Deliverables

The Problem

Language learning is a journey that many embark on, but few complete. The process requires immense time, dedication, and intensive practice. Most importantly, users often report a crippling fear of speaking out loud to native speakers simply because they lack the conversational confidence. Current apps focus too heavily on vocabulary memorization rather than conversational application.

User Insights

After conducting 15+ user interviews and surveys, I found a recurring theme: learners feel entirely disconnected from real-world conversation. They memorize flashcards efficiently but freeze mechanically when placed in a dynamic dialogue setup. This insight became the north-star driving Converseo's AI conversational methodology.

Project Goals

  • Design a frictionless onboarding funnel for novice learners.
  • Build an engaging interface that feels like a messaging app to lower speaking anxiety.
  • Implement a subtle but effective gamification loop.

The Solution

A beautifully stripped-back interface that places entirely dynamic, AI-generated conversational agents front and center. The design relies heavily on familiar chatting patterns paired with deep contextual audio feedback, bridging the gap between learning rules and executing them confidently.

Key Design Decisions

💬 Familiar Chat UI

To reduce cognitive load and anxiety, the main learning environment heavily mimics popular messaging apps, bringing instant familiarity to the user.

🎯 Guided Prompts

For beginners, a completely open-ended AI chat can be daunting. Floating contextual prompts suggest vocabulary without penalizing users for picking them.

🎨 Gentle Color Palette

Bright neon language apps often increase heart rate and urgency. I settled on muted, grounding colors to reinforce an environment of patience and calm.
