A Little About Me

Hello there 👋, thank's for getting to this point in my Portfolio. A little introduction, my name is Ruth Ezeneche and my dream is to be a really good Design Engineer. My journey into design started with a desire to create.

With a degree in Human Resources Management and years of experience in Customer service, sales and HR, I have worked in people facing roles for the most of my career and have learnt over time to understand people and anticipate their needs. That experience shaped how I think about design today, with empathy, clarity, and a strong focus on usability.
Now, I design and build products using tools like Figma, Bubble, and Webflow, with a growing interest in frontend development. I’m especially interested in the space between design and development, where ideas turn into real, usable experiences.

My biggest goal remains continuous self-improvement, striving to refine my craft and to evolve as a designer and developer.
When I'm not working, I enjoy reading, baking, practicing my (very questionable) French, dancing salsa, and traveling.
